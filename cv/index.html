Benedict Jones

Benedict is a Senior Data Scientist at Evergreen Life, focusing on leveraging machine learning to improve the health and wellbeing in the UK. Benedict has a background in Engineering, graduating with an MEng in 2019. Following this he went on to complete a PhD at the Department of Engineering at Durham University with a research focuses on Unconventional Computing.

Research Interests

RAG powered LLMs

Leveraging Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) and novel retrieval methods to deliver informed Large Language Model (LLM) powered insights and chat interactions.

Electronic Health Records

Using longitudinal health records to predict negative outcomes. AI explainability techniques mean can then begin to understand what factors and preventative measures contribute to better future health.

Machine Learning Interpretability

Exploring methods (a.k.a 'AI explainability') to make complex ML models more transparent and explainable, including SHAP values.

Unconventional Computing

Exploiting complex dynamic systems for low-power, efficient novel computation strategies. Work focused on Evolution in-Materio, which included Neuroevolution, Reservoir Computing, and Extreme Learning Machines.
